What changes the price (be honest)

  • Condition: heavy limescale, mould, heavy grease, neglect.
  • Scope add-ons: inside oven/fridge/cupboards, blinds, multiple internal windows.
  • Access/time constraints: key collection, no parking, stairs only, tight deadlines.
  • Size: beds/baths matter more than “small/medium/large”.
