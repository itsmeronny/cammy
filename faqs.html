Frequently Asked Questions

Most issues are simple. Read this first. If you still need help, use the support form below and be specific.

Pricing clarity Defined scopes Nationwide UK Quality-led
Are your cleaners insured? ▾
Yes. Cleaners are required to have (or be able to obtain) public liability insurance appropriate to the work.
Do you operate nationwide? ▾
Yes. We cover major UK cities and surrounding areas. Availability depends on demand and cleaner supply in your postcode.
Do I need to provide cleaning products? ▾
Usually no. Most cleaners bring their own products and equipment. If you have preferences (eco-only, fragrance-free, etc.), tell us upfront.
What’s the difference between a deep clean and end of tenancy? ▾
  • Deep clean: a thorough one-off reset (good for lived-in homes).
  • End of tenancy: a checklist-driven clean aimed at move-out standard (often includes inside cupboards, skirting, detailed kitchen/bathroom).
Do you clean ovens, fridges, and inside cupboards? ▾
It depends on the service and what you select. For end of tenancy, these are commonly included or available as add-ons. If you want specific internals cleaned, state it clearly.
Can I book a same-day or next-day clean? ▾
Sometimes. It depends on your postcode and cleaner availability. If it’s urgent, submit a request with your postcode and the service type.
How do you price jobs? ▾
Pricing is based on service type + property size + condition + access/time constraints. The fastest way to get a firm price is to provide: postcode, bedrooms, bathrooms, and any heavy soiling/problem areas.
What if I’m not happy with the result? ▾
Raise it immediately with specifics (photos help). If something was missed within the agreed scope, we’ll arrange a remedy where reasonable.
Can I leave keys / use key safe access? ▾
Yes. Key safes, concierge/porter access, or arranged key handovers are common. Provide instructions clearly in the request.
Do you do commercial and office cleaning? ▾
Yes. Provide premises type, size (sq ft if known), frequency required, and any out-of-hours constraints.
