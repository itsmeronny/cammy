About CamCleans

CamCleans is a nationwide cleaning network focused on predictable results: clear scopes, clean communication, and quality you can measure.

What we do

We match customers to vetted cleaning professionals for domestic and commercial jobs, with consistent scope and quality expectations.

What we don’t do

No vague “we’ll see on the day”. If the condition is heavier than described, we re-scope before proceeding.

How quoting works

Price is driven by service type, size, condition, and access/time constraints. Photos = faster, firmer quotes.

Quality standard

Defined checklist expectations. If you want extras (inside appliances, heavy limescale, mould), declare it upfront.

Core principles

  • Scope first: no surprises, no silent exclusions.
  • Fast routing: postcode + job type determines availability.
  • Quality-led: checklists and touch-point standards.
  • Honest constraints: heavy soil, mould, or neglect changes time and price.
