After Builders Cleaning

Renovation and construction work leaves behind fine dust, residue and surface debris that standard cleaning does not remove. Our after builders cleaning service restores properties to a clean, safe and presentable condition following refurbishment.

What Is Included

  • Fine dust removal from walls, ceilings and fixtures
  • Detailed cleaning of newly fitted kitchens and bathrooms
  • Window frame, sill and track cleaning
  • Paint splatter and residue removal where safely possible
  • Floor vacuuming and hard floor mopping
  • Surface sanitisation throughout

Why Post-Renovation Cleaning Matters

Construction dust settles into ventilation areas, corners and high-level fittings. If not removed properly, it can continue circulating for weeks. A structured post-build clean ensures the property is safe for occupation and visually ready for handover.

Suitable For

  • Homeowners after renovation projects
  • Property developers preparing homes for sale
  • Contractors completing build handovers
  • Commercial refurbishments
