End of Tenancy Cleaning

Moving out of a rental property requires more than a quick clean. Our end of tenancy cleaning service is designed to meet landlord and letting agent expectations, helping tenants maximise deposit return while preparing properties for immediate re-letting.

What Is Included

  • Full kitchen deep clean including cupboard interiors
  • Oven interior degreasing and detailing
  • Bathroom descaling, grout cleaning and sanitisation
  • Skirting boards, switches and door frames wiped down
  • Internal window cleaning
  • Full property vacuum and hard floor mopping

Our Cleaning Standard

We follow a structured checklist to ensure consistency across every property. Attention is given to high-touch areas, hidden dust zones and appliance interiors. This is not a surface-level clean — it is a detailed reset of the property.

Who This Service Is For

This service is ideal for:

  • Tenants preparing for property inspection
  • Landlords between tenancies
  • Letting agents managing portfolio properties
  • Property investors preparing units for sale

Nationwide Coverage

CamCleans operates across major UK cities with vetted cleaning professionals. Availability may vary by location. Contact us for confirmation in your area.
